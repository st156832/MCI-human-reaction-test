Human Capabilities - Reaction test

Developed by Lukas Czurgel(3349234), Glarin Langston(3470129), Eduard-Christian Talos(3589379) as part of the human-computer interactions (MCI) course of the university of Stuttgart, Germany.

What is this?

The test consists of 3 different experiments, each measuring a different facet of human reaction time. The order of experiments as well as the experiments themselves are randomized.

How does this work?

by clicking the "get started" button below, you will be presented with the first of three experiments. Instruction for each experiment are displayed on screen. We ask participant to absolve 30 rounds per experiment in order to get a comparable sample size. Once the experiment is done, instructions for the next experiment will be displayed on screen.

Will my data get stored?

No, GitHub pages does not currently support backend integration. As such we cannot store your data in a database or file. Participants are thus asked to report heir results to us on the ilias thread.
